Meet Our Staff

Our Manager Mickey Mouse

Mickey Mouse has been a central part of the Disneyeat team since joining in 2011 – where he originally planned to help out for a few weeks with the reception team. Seven years later Mickey oversees the entire Disneyeat operation. Mickey Mouse has an exceptional eye for detail, a wonderful approach to hospitality and is now on first name terms with the majority of Disneyeat guests.

Our Head-Chef Pluto

Our Head Chef Pluto joined Disneyeat in January 2017 as Junior Sous Chef having spent 3 years at Gordon Ramsay’s 3 Michelin Star restaurant Royal Hospital Road along with spells at Petrus Restaurant in Knightsbridge and Yauatcha in Soho. Pluto is an incredibly driven and passionate member of the team who dreamed of becoming a chef from a young age following holidays in France and cooking alongside her best friends Pippo. Above all else Pluto is a foodie who loves to eat out in her spare time.

Our Sommelier Pippo

Pippos' great passion is for wines; he is an IHK Certified Sommelier, studied at the Wine Academy in Rust (A) and received the WSET diploma and the title of 'Weinakademiker' (Wine Academy). He is also a Certified Sommelier by the renowned Court of Master Sommeliers.

Our Cake Design Minnie

Her best quotes: "pastry is my way of communicating, my tool of creativity." Minnie is another leading cake artist who has chosen by the Cake Masters as one of the Top 10 Cake Artists in America. For 2017 as well as featured on the cover page of Cake Master Magazine. Her hobby of cake baking and decorations became her passion. She recognizes the potential of her hobby and takes her interest to a new level. In Disneyeat she turns imagination into reality by taking theme based cake orders.
